Here is a recipe on how to make my classic grilled cheese:

Description:

This grilled cheese is excellent. I have been making it as a snack for many years. It's quick, and when done correctly, tastes incredible.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 slices of bread
  2. As much gruyere cheese as your heart desires
  3. A quarter of an onion
  4. Half stick of butter

Instructions:

  1. Dice up the onion
  2. Heat up the pan at around medium levels
  3. Once heated, place butter in the pan
  4. After butter melts, place bread in pan individually for about a minute to sear
  5. Remove bread, assemble grilled cheese without onions
  6. Place grilled cheese in pan for 4 minutes, then flip
  7. Use roof on pan to melt cheese
  8. Place onions inside grilled cheese
  9. Place one slice of each on the outside of both slices of bread to achieve a cheesy crust
  10. Sear both sides in pan for 2 minutes on high heat
  11. Remove and let it sit on a plate for 2 minutes
  12. Enjoy!
